About Our Club

We are a non-profit organization willing to take the initiative to educate our generation. Our passion for coding has driven us to assist younger students to develop their interest in this vast field of technology.

We are a team of three developers who have worked together on several projects in the past. Our team consists of a web developer, an app developer, and 2 hardware developers.

Our goal is to create a safe and efficient learning environment that allows our students to succeed and become benificial members of society tomorrow. By doing so, we are helping the future generation innovate new pieces of technology.
